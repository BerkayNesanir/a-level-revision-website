Economics (A2)

Economics is a social science and many solutions would be acceptable in different economic situations. You must be able to explain why one change would lead to a certain outcome and evaluate any other potential consequences of the change. Understanding why these changes lead to certain outcomes is more useful than memorising the chain of events brought by the change becuase applying it to more complex situations would be easier if you have a sufficient understanding of the fundamentals.

What to expect:

  • Understanding of concepts
  • Limitations of concepts
  • Explanations of chains of events
  • Useful diagrams

Content: Theme 3 and Theme 4

Level: A2
