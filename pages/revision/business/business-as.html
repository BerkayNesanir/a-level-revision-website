A-Level Support

Business (AS)

Businesses make decisions and adopt strategies which help them meet their aims and objectives. Based on a given company's situation you would need to advise them to ensure they reach their desired outcome. You would need to learn the advantages and potential disadvantages of business concepts and explain them in varying contexts. A clear understanding of these concepts can make it easier to form succinct chains of reasoning which can increase your exam scores.

What to expect:

  • Understanding of concepts
  • Limitations of concepts
  • Explanations of chains of events
  • Useful diagrams

Content: Theme 1 and Theme 2

Level: AS

Exam board: Edexcel
