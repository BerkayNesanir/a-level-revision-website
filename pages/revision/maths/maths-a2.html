Mathematics (A2)

When learning or revising mathematics it is more important to understand why a method or process works than it is to simply memorise it. This would not only help you retain information longer but also make it much easier for you to successfully answer harder exam questions.

What to expect:

  • Key formulae
  • Process of answering questions
  • Understanding of methods
  • Worked examples
